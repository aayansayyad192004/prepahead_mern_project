# 🎯 PrepAhead: Placement Preparation Platform

PrepAhead is an AI-powered **Placement Preparation Platform** designed to supercharge your job hunt and upskill journey. From personalized job and course recommendations to mock interviews and mentorship, PrepAhead provides an all-in-one solution to help students and professionals land their dream jobs.

## 🔗 Project Link  
🚀 [PrepAhead](https://prepahead-mern-project.onrender.com/)

---

## 🌟 Overview  
PrepAhead is revolutionizing placement preparation by integrating **AI-powered tools** and **personalized learning** paths. Whether you're a student gearing up for campus placements or a professional exploring new opportunities, PrepAhead equips you with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to succeed.

Using cutting-edge AI, the platform offers:
- Personalized job and course recommendations  
- AI-generated roadmaps tailored to your goals  
- Mock interviews and skill assessments  
- Mentorship opportunities with chat and video call options  
- Secure payment gateway for mentorship sessions  

---

## ✨ Key Features  

- 💼 **Personalized Job Recommendations**  
  Receive job suggestions based on your skills, interests, and career goals.

- 📚 **AI-Powered Course Suggestions**  
  Get AI-curated learning paths and course recommendations to upskill efficiently.

- 🎙️ **Mock Interviews**  
  Experience realistic AI-driven mock interviews with instant feedback to boost your confidence.

- 🧠 **Skill Assessments & Analysis**  
  Take assessments to identify strengths and areas for improvement, with detailed reports.

- 🗺️ **Roadmap Generation**  
  AI-generated custom career roadmaps that align with your goals and skills.

- 👤 **Profile Setup & Management**  
  Create and manage a professional profile that helps mentors and recruiters find you.

- 🤝 **Mentorship Opportunities**  
  Connect with industry experts for personalized mentorship.  
  ➤ Options include **Chat** or **Video Call** sessions.  
  ➤ **Razorpay Payment Integration** for mentorship sessions.

- 💳 **Secure Payment Gateway**  
  Integrated **Razorpay** to handle transactions for Mentorship Section.

- 🔒 **Secure Authentication**  
  Seamless and secure login with **Firebase Authentication**.
